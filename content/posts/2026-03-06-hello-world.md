I have wanted to write publicly for a long time. I have kept notes, half-finished essays, and ideas scattered across too many apps for years. This is the push I needed.

## What This Blog Is

Three things, roughly:

**1. Technical writing** — Notes from my work in machine learning. When I figure something out that took me longer than it should have, I will write it down here. Paper summaries, implementation notes, things that tripped me up. Mostly for myself, but publicly, so maybe it helps someone else.

**2. Research thinking** — I spend a lot of time thinking about how neural networks learn and represent knowledge. Sometimes I want to think out loud about that, outside of the constraints of a formal paper.

**3. Everything else** — I also write fiction. Personal essays. Thoughts about films, books, games. I am not going to keep that separate from the technical writing. This is one blog, not a brand.

## Who I Am

I am a Lecturer at BRAC University and a researcher in deep learning. My MSc thesis at the University of Dhaka looked at how artificial neural networks encapsulate knowledge — how they compress, store, and generalise information from training data. Before that, I spent time as an ML Engineer building practical AI systems: RAG pipelines, LLM reasoning tools, computer vision and ASR fine-tuning.

I also watch too many films and read too little fiction, which I am trying to fix.

## What to Expect

No fixed schedule. I will write when I have something to say. You can subscribe via [RSS](/index.xml) if you want to be notified.

If you want to reach me, links are on the [Portfolio](/portfolio/) page.
